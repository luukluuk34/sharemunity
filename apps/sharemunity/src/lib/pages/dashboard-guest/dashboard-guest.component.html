How does it work?

Sharemunity is a website for people who like lending and borrowing items of choice from friends and families. It also gives an overview of who has what items because if you borrow and lend out often you can lose track!

Join Communities

Once registered go to the communities page and look for a community created by your friends or one that has your liking and press the join button!

Search for an item!

Once you are in a community you can browse for all items people are willing to lend out.

Reserve the item

Found the book you where looking for? Or the tools you need for your DIY project? Ask if you can borrow it by reservation!

Borrow the item!

Has the reservation been accepted? on the agreed upon day go to your friend and borrow the item. Make sure to return it in time! and enjoy!
